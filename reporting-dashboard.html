Case

To continuesly follow your work, data is critical, to control what you're doing & how well that delivers against your targets. For B2B they didn't have the capability or solution to do this effectively. Me and a developer helped them launch a dashboard so they could report on key KPI:s.

The dashboard didn't only help them report but they also learned more about the business area. With the dashboard they accessed information about donations where the payment via invoice never was payed - meaning, they could contact the giver & remind them to pay the invoice so the donation was donated. This resulted in increase of revenue.
