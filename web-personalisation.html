Case

In todays digital world people want a great experiences when visiting a website or receiving a marketing message - the two should be tied together. At Save the Children our marketing and website was generic. The experience or content wasn't tailored to any specific campaign or segment.

As a Product Owner of web I worked with a solution with the web team where you could utilize visitor groups and campaign codes to personalise the content on specific pages via Optimizely.

The work resulted in an increase of monthly givers due to that business could separate what content a segment would see, tailor it to the specific marketing message and even send out physical products as a result of becoming a monthly giver.
