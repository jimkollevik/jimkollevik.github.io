Case

In non-profit a donation is valuable. Sometimes more valuable than data. But, the attention & dedication of a potential giver can change rapidly & you need to make it extremely simple to make a donation. I & the web team launched a super-easy way of giving, where you choose the pre-shown amount & pay via phone, in less than 20 seconds.

The solution broke even after 14 days & reached seven figures after one & half month. The solution then stood for 50% of the revenue for one off donations after it was released.
