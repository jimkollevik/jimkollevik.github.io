Case

At Save the Children Sweden we had different ways of giving. One of the ways is a postal letter which talked to the giver about the work we were doing and asked for an one off donation.

The marketing department wanted to optimize the segmentation of the target group and increase the revenue and response rate from the product.

IT worked with marketing to implement a RFM model in the data layer and push that data to the reporting tool & Marketing Automation tool so marketing could segment on the model in both tools. The RFM model and implementation increased the response rate with 20% and number of givers increased with 16%. The revenue increased and the cost decreased.
