Case

At Save the Children Sweden we had different ways of giving. One of them was a postal letter which talked to the giver about the work we were doing & asked for an one off donation. The marketing department wanted to optimize the segmentation of the target group & increase the revenue & response rate from the product.

IT worked with marketing to implement a RFM model in the data layer & push that data to the reporting tool & Marketing Automation tool so marketing could segment on the RFM score in both tools. The RFM model & implementation increased the response rate with 20% & number of givers increased with 16%. The revenue increased as well & the cost decreased.
