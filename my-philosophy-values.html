Philosophy

When working with IT, System as a product or technology it's easy to have a strong mindset of tech and what it can do for you. My take on technology is that it's an enabler. Whether that be digital transformation, sending emails, have a 360 look of your customers or just help you plan a project. But the technology isn't the solution itself, it's a tool to help you advance.

We have technology and systems all around us which helps us do different tasks and this is how technology should be use, to help users, humans accelerate and transform their businesses. For me humans and users comes first, with their problems and processes, technology is second.

Values

We live in a complex world and society. It's never been more easy to create a website and start a business. We have social media channels, information and data are flowing and created faster and at larger scale than ever before.

My value in life and work is to make the world, society and business simplier and more agile. What I mean is that, when I take on a project or get a technical brief, my first thought is: How can I simplify this solution. By doing that I get rid of the unneccessary and focus on the core value. It saves time, resources and in the end money.
