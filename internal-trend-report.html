Side project

I love the Google vizualisation they've done regarding hot search trends. It got me thinking that maybe I could make my own hot trend report and insert data from different internal data sources.

I started brainstorming by myself about fun data sources you easily could display, such as top travel destinations, top rented cars, top purchased items in an e-commerce site. This is a prototype to display top travel destinations from a fictive travel agency.
