Side project

For long as I remember I always been a curious person & eager to learn more about things. Together with that I want to grow as a person, both professionally & personally. When I studied & starting working I began to reflect on my days & achievements. I collected insights from my work, personal experiences & by talking to people asking for feedback.

I saved them mentally but quite often I forgot about many of them. I needed a tool to help me keep track of my learnings where I could store them & go back and read them again, to repeat the learning.

I created what I call "My Learning Canvas" where I, each day or week, reflect upon what I've learned & write them down. I use a google spreadsheet to do so & it has helped me keep track of my learnings.

You can download a copy of the spreadsheet under "Resources".

My role

Ask for feedback
Reflect to get insights
Discover a tool to save insights
