Case

To rent a car is quite straight forward. You visit the website and choose the car you want and when you need it. From a business stand point that's direct conversion. At Hertz we needed to attract customers who were a step before a conversion. In order to do that we had to create different contents for those types of web searches.

The CMS had its limitations which hindered us to create a platform for the new content. I transformed a default web template into a responsive platform for the new content and the digital agency optimized the content for web searches.
