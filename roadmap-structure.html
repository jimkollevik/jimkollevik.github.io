Case

During my time as a Product Owner I've created several different planning and roadmaps for different systems and IT products. I've tried alternatives such as Powerpoint, Miro, Jira and Excel. The difficulty I've faced is to not make it to detailed nor to high level. Another aspect is also for the business end to understand and be part of the planning.

I've chosed to display the roadmaps I've done in Miro because it highlights the difficulty of being a Product Owner for several systems and products but also to be clear what should be done and when. Miro is quite easy to work with once you create and template. I chosed to categorize the work in four different cateories: Infrastructure, Operations, Estimates and new developments. Why I did this is because we needed to highlight it to the organization (which was heavy on the new developments) that there are other aspects which takes time from the team. I then created a backlog of items in excel, both from the business side and development side. We then, together, prioritized what had to be done first and estimated the time. After that I could plot the pieces together and get acceptance from the business and my team of the roadmap.

You can see the resultat under resources. What is missing from this roadmap, in my mind, is the vision. Which was very hard to get out of the business side.
