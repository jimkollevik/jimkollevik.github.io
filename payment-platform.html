Case

At Save the children I was the product owner for the payment products. During that time we got notice from our card supplier that they were to terminate the platform we was on. Which made us to create a project to look for a new supplier and platform.

My role was product owner and I was responsible to lead the work. I gathered requirements from primarely business and development but also economics. After that I then searched for alternative suppliers which fulfilled the requirements and they held presentations.

Success of this work was to work together but also update our platform and future-proof it. We selected the same supplier as we had but implemented their new platform. It was an successful launch in the sense that right people was involved and we future-proofed the solution.
